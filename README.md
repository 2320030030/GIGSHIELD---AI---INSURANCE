#  GigShield – AI Insurance for Delivery Workers

##  Problem Statement

Gig workers (Zomato, Swiggy, Blinkit delivery partners) lose 20–30% of their income due to external disruptions such as heavy rain, extreme heat, and pollution. Currently, there is no system to protect their income during such events.

---

##  Our Solution

GigShield is an **AI-powered parametric insurance platform** that automatically compensates delivery workers when disruptions occur.

This system provides:

* Weekly insurance coverage
* AI-based premium calculation
* Automatic claim triggering
* Instant payout simulation

---

##  Target Users

* Food delivery workers (Zomato / Swiggy)
* Daily wage-based gig workers
* Workers affected by environmental disruptions

---

##  System Workflow

1. User registers on the platform
2. AI calculates **weekly premium based on risk**
3. User activates weekly insurance policy
4. System monitors environmental conditions
5. If disruption occurs → claim is automatically triggered
6. Worker receives payout instantly

---

##  Weekly Pricing Model

Premium is calculated weekly based on:

* Location risk
* Weather conditions
* Historical disruption data

**Example:**

* Low Risk → ₹20/week
* Medium Risk → ₹30/week
* High Risk → ₹50/week

---

##  AI Integration

* **Risk Prediction:** Predicts likelihood of disruption
* **Dynamic Pricing:** Adjusts weekly premium
* **Fraud Detection:** Prevents fake claims using anomaly detection

---

##  Parametric Triggers

| Event        | Condition |
| ------------ | --------- |
| Heavy Rain   | > 50 mm   |
| Extreme Heat | > 40°C    |
| Pollution    | AQI > 300 |

👉 When triggered → **automatic claim + payout**
